Jiri Sedlar

I'm a research scientist at the Czech Institute of Informatics, Robotics and Cybernetics (CIIRC), Czech Technical University in Prague, in the IMPACT group led by Josef Sivic.

I did my PhD at Charles University / Czech Academy of Sciences in Prague, where I was advised by Jan Flusser, followed by postdoc stays at Flinders University in Adelaide, Australia, and Mayo Clinic in Rochester, MN, USA.
